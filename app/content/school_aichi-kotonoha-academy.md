Aichi Kotonoha Academy offers comprehensive courses tailored to meet the diverse needs of international students. The curriculum emphasizes improving all four language skills: reading, writing, listening, and speaking. They place a strong emphasis on practical conversation skills for daily life and academic pursuits. The school also provides focused preparation for the EJU (Examination for Japanese University Admission for International Students) and the JLPT (Japanese-Language Proficiency Test).

*   **Beginner:** Basic Japanese grammar, vocabulary, and conversational skills. (3-6 months. Focus: Hiragana, Katakana, basic sentence structure, greetings, self-introduction.)
