## 🏫 University Overview
Kurume University (久留米大学, Kurume Daigaku) is a prestigious private university located in the heart of Kurume City, Fukuoka Prefecture, on the island of Kyushu. Established in 1928, it originally began as Kyushu Medical School, reflecting its deeply rooted expertise in the medical sciences. Over the nearly 100 years since its founding, it has expanded into a comprehensive institution that bridges the gap between traditional medicine and the modern social sciences. 

For international students, Kurume University represents a strategic choice. It offers the academic rigor of a top-tier private institution without the overwhelming congestion and high living costs of Tokyo or Osaka. The university operates two primary campuses: the Asahimachi Campus, which is the hub for medical research and the university hospital, and the Mii Campus, a sprawling green environment where the humanities, law, economics, and commerce departments are located. This dual-campus structure allows students to specialize in their fields while being part of a larger, interdisciplinary community.

## 🎓 Academic Excellence and Faculty Breakdown

### 🩺 Faculty of Medicine
The crown jewel of Kurume University is undoubtedly its Faculty of Medicine. It is one of the oldest and most respected private medical schools in Japan. The faculty is known for producing high-caliber doctors and researchers who lead the healthcare sector in Kyushu. The associated Kurume University Hospital is one of the largest medical facilities in the region, providing students with unparalleled clinical training opportunities. For international students, entry into the medical faculty is extremely competitive and requires near-native Japanese proficiency.

### ⚖️ Faculty of Law and Faculty of Literature
The Faculty of Law offers specialized tracks in judicial studies, public administration, and international business law. It is particularly popular among students aiming for civil service or legal professions in Japan. Meanwhile, the Faculty of Literature provides a deep dive into Japanese language, psychology, and intercultural communication. For international students, the Japanese Language and Culture track is an excellent way to integrate into the local society while gaining a formal degree.

### 📈 Faculty of Economics and Faculty of Commerce
These faculties are the engines of the university’s social science division. The Faculty of Economics focuses on regional revitalization and global economic trends, while the Faculty of Commerce emphasizes practical business skills, accounting, and marketing. Kurume University has strong ties with the local business community in Fukuoka, which is one of Japan's fastest-growing startup and business hubs.

### 🏃 Faculty of Human Health
Established to meet the needs of an aging society, this faculty focuses on sports science, social welfare, and health management. It combines physical education with psychological support, preparing students for roles in healthcare management and professional coaching.

## 🌏 International Student Environment and Support
Kurume University takes a proactive approach to internationalization. The Center for International Education is the primary support system for foreign students. 

### Japanese Language Support
Unlike some universities that leave international students to fend for themselves, Kurume provides dedicated Japanese language support. For those who need to bolster their language skills before or during their degree programs, the university offers elective courses in Japanese business etiquette, academic writing, and JLPT (Japanese Language Proficiency Test) preparation.

### Global Exchange Programs
The university maintains partnerships with over 30 institutions worldwide, including universities in the United States, United Kingdom, China, and Korea. This global network fosters a diverse campus atmosphere where local Japanese students are accustomed to interacting with international peers.

## 💰 Tuition and Financial Aid
One of the most attractive aspects of Kurume University for international students is its affordability compared to urban private universities.

- **Humanities and Social Sciences:** The total first-year cost (including admission fees and tuition) typically ranges from 1,100,000 JPY to 1,250,000 JPY. Subsequent years are cheaper as the admission fee is a one-time payment.
- **Medicine:** As with all private medical schools in Japan, the tuition is significantly higher, often exceeding 5,000,000 JPY per year.

### Scholarships
Kurume University offers several internal scholarship programs specifically for international students who demonstrate academic excellence and financial need. These include:
1. **Tuition Reduction Program:** A 30% to 50% waiver on tuition fees for eligible international students.
2. **Kurume University Scholarship:** A monthly stipend for high-achieving students.
3. **MEXT (Monbukagakusho) Honors Scholarship:** Recommendations for national-level funding are available for top-tier candidates.

## 🏘️ Life in Kurume: The Student Experience
Choosing Kurume University means choosing the 'Kurume Life.' Kurume is a mid-sized city that offers a perfect balance between urban convenience and natural beauty. 

### Cost of Living
The cost of living in Kurume is roughly 30-40% lower than in Tokyo. Rent for a standard studio apartment near the Mii Campus ranges from 30,000 JPY to 50,000 JPY per month. Groceries and local dining are also significantly more affordable, with the city being world-famous as the birthplace of 'Tonkotsu Ramen.'

### Campus Facilities
The Mii Campus features a modern library, high-tech computer labs, and extensive athletic facilities. The university cafeteria is a popular social hub, offering nutritious meals for under 500 JPY. For medical students at the Asahimachi Campus, the proximity to the Chikugo River provides a serene environment for study and relaxation.

## 📝 Admission Guide for International Students
Admission to Kurume University typically follows the standard Japanese academic calendar, with the school year beginning in April. 

### Entry Requirements
1. **EJU (Examination for Japanese University Admission for International Students):** Most faculties require scores from the Japanese as a Foreign Language, Japan and the World, and Mathematics sections.
2. **English Proficiency:** While primarily a Japanese-taught institution, submitting TOEIC or TOEFL scores can be advantageous, particularly for the Faculty of Commerce and Economics.
3. **Internal Examination:** This usually consists of a short essay (in Japanese) and an interview. The interview is a crucial component where professors assess the student's motivation and communication skills.

### Application Timeline
- **September - October:** Application period for the first round of admissions.
- **November:** Entrance examinations (Interview and Essay).
- **December:** Results announcement.
- **April:** Entrance ceremony.

## 🚀 Career and Future Prospects
Kurume University has an impressive employment rate, often exceeding 95% for its graduates. The university’s Career Support Center provides specialized guidance for international students, including mock interviews, resume workshops tailored to Japanese standards, and job fairs featuring local Kyushu-based companies.

Because Fukuoka is a gateway to Asia, many graduates find opportunities in international logistics, trade, and tourism. Furthermore, the strong reputation of the medical and social science faculties ensures that a degree from Kurume University is well-respected across Japan.

## 💡 Conclusion: Is Kurume University Right for You?
Kurume University is an ideal destination for international students who are looking for a high-quality education in a supportive, regional environment. It is particularly suited for those who:
- Want to master the Japanese language and immerse themselves in Japanese culture.
- Are looking for a lower cost of living without sacrificing academic prestige.
- Aim to build a career in the Kyushu region or in medical/social welfare fields.
- Prefer a close-knit campus community over the anonymity of massive urban universities.

By choosing Kurume University, you are not just choosing a school; you are choosing a community that has been a cornerstone of Japanese education for nearly a century. Whether you are aiming for the cutting edge of medical science or the complexities of global economics, Kurume University provides the foundation necessary to succeed in the 21st-century global landscape.
