## School Overview

Kobe YMCA College of Professional Studies (神戸YMCA学院専門学校) has a long-standing tradition of providing quality Japanese language education to international students. As part of the global YMCA network, the school emphasizes holistic development, focusing not only on language acquisition but also on cultural understanding and personal growth. The campus atmosphere is generally described as welcoming and supportive, with a diverse student body from various countries, fostering a vibrant international learning environment. The school's mission is to empower students with the Japanese language skills and cultural knowledge needed to succeed in their academic or professional pursuits in Japan.

## Location & Surroundings

Located in the heart of Kobe, Kobe YMCA College of Professional Studies offers excellent access to various amenities and transportation hubs. Situated in Chuo-ku (中央区), the school is close to shopping districts, restaurants, and cultural attractions, making it a convenient and exciting place to study. The neighborhood is generally considered safe and well-maintained. Convenience stores (konbini) are readily available in the area, ensuring students can easily access daily necessities.

*   **Sannomiya Station (三宮駅):** 5-10 minutes walk
*   **Kobe Station (神戸駅):** 15-20 minutes walk/train
*   **Shin-Kobe Station (新神戸駅):** 10-15 minutes by subway

## Curriculum & Courses

Kobe YMCA College of Professional Studies offers a comprehensive range of Japanese language courses tailored to different proficiency levels. The curriculum is designed to prepare students for various goals, including further education at Japanese universities and vocational schools, as well as career opportunities in Japan. Courses include thorough preparation for the Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) and the Examination for Japanese University Admission for International Students (EJU). The school emphasizes practical communication skills, particularly conversational Japanese, alongside grammar and reading comprehension.

*   **Spring Beginner Course:** Acquire basic Japanese for daily conversation. Admission in April, duration varies.
*   **Spring Intermediate/Advanced Course:** Improve fluency for academic purposes. Admission in April, duration varies.
*   **Autumn Beginner Course:** Acquire basic Japanese for daily conversation. Admission in October, duration varies.
*   **Autumn Intermediate/Advanced Course:** Improve fluency for academic purposes. Admission in October, duration varies.
*   **2-Year Course:** Comprehensive Japanese language program for university preparation. Admission in April, duration is 2 years.

In addition to core language classes, Kobe YMCA College of Professional Studies offers elective courses that allow students to specialize in areas of interest. These may include:

*   **Business Japanese:** Focuses on business etiquette, terminology, and communication skills essential for working in a Japanese company.
*   **Japanese Culture:** Explores various aspects of Japanese culture, such as traditional arts, history, and social customs.
*   **Art & Design:** This appears to cater to students wanting to enter art and design-related fields in Japan.
