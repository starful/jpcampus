Established in 1874 by American Methodist missionaries, it boasts a rich history and a strong reputation for academic excellence, particularly in the fields of business, international studies, and law. Aoyama Gakuin is consistently ranked among the top private universities in Japan and is highly regarded for its modern facilities, international outlook, and strong connections to the business world. It actively encourages international students to study at its campuses and provides a range of support services to ensure a smooth transition and successful academic experience. The university is also known for its active alumni network, which provides valuable career opportunities and mentorship for graduates.

The university emphasizes a holistic education that fosters critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a strong sense of social responsibility. It is committed to producing graduates who are well-prepared to contribute to a globalized society.
