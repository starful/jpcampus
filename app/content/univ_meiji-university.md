## University Overview

Meiji University, often called Meidai, is a prestigious and historically important private university in Japan. Established in 1881 by three young lawyers—Tatsuo Kishimoto, Kozo Miyagi, and Misao Yashiro—Meiji University was founded during a period of rapid modernization and Westernization in Japan. It was designed as a liberal and progressive institution, offering legal education to a wider range of students than traditional institutions. The university quickly became known for its academic excellence and dedication to promoting independent thought and critical inquiry. Meiji University played a key role in shaping modern Japanese society, producing many leaders in politics, business, and culture.

Today, Meiji University is a comprehensive university with multiple campuses across Tokyo, offering a wide variety of undergraduate and graduate programs. It consistently ranks among the top universities in Japan and is recognized for its strong academic programs, research output, and global engagement. The university is dedicated to providing high-quality education that prepares students for success in a rapidly changing world. It also participates in the Top Global University Project (SGU), highlighting its commitment to internationalization and the development of globally competent graduates.

## International Student Support

Meiji University offers a comprehensive range of support services for international students to ensure a smooth transition and a fulfilling academic experience. These services include:

*   **Dormitory:** Meiji University provides on-campus and off-campus dormitory options for international students, offering a safe and convenient living environment.
*   **English Programs:** Many faculties offer courses taught in English, enabling international students to pursue their studies without Japanese language proficiency. The university also provides Japanese language courses to help students improve their language skills.
*   **Career Support:** The university's career center offers career counseling, job search assistance, and internship opportunities to help international students achieve their career goals. They also host career fairs and workshops specifically for international students.
*   **Scholarships:** Meiji University offers a variety of scholarships for international students, based on academic merit and financial need. Information about scholarships is available on the university's website.
*   **International Student Support Office:** The International Student Support Office provides a wide range of services, including visa assistance, orientation programs, and cultural events. This office serves as a central point of contact for international students and helps them navigate university life.

## Campus Location & Access

Meiji University has four main campuses, each offering a unique learning environment. Here's a summary of campus locations and access information:

*   **Surugadai:**
    *   Location: Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo
    *   Access: Ochanomizu Station (JR Chuo/Sobu Lines), Jimbocho Station (Toei Mita/Shinjuku/Hanzomon Lines), Ogawamachi Station.
*   **Izumi:**
    *   Location: Suginami-ku, Tokyo
    *   Access: Meidaimae Station (Keio Line, Keio Inokashira Line)
*   **Ikuta:**
    *   Location: Tama-ku, Kawasaki, Kanagawa Prefecture
    *   Access: Mukogaokayuen Station (Odakyu Odawara Line)
*   **Nakano:**
    *   Location: Nakano-ku, Tokyo
    *   Access: Nakano Station (JR Chuo/Sobu Lines, Tokyo Metro Tozai Line)

_The Surugadai Campus is the main campus and houses many of the undergraduate faculties. The Izumi campus is mainly for first and second-year students. The Ikuta campus focuses on science and technology, and the Nakano campus hosts international studies and global governance programs._
