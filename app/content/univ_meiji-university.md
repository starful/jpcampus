## üè´ University Overview

Meiji University, or Meidai as it is commonly known, is one of Japan's most prestigious and historically significant private universities. Founded in 1881 by three young lawyers, Tatsuo Kishimoto, Kozo Miyagi, and Misao Yashiro, Meiji University was established during a period of rapid modernization and Westernization in Japan. It was conceived as a liberal and progressive institution, providing legal education to a broader range of students than traditional institutions. The university quickly gained a reputation for academic excellence and a commitment to fostering independent thought and critical inquiry. Meiji University played a significant role in shaping modern Japanese society, producing numerous leaders in politics, business, and culture.

Today, Meiji University stands as a comprehensive university with multiple campuses across Tokyo, offering a wide array of undergraduate and graduate programs. It is consistently ranked among the top universities in Japan, recognized for its strong academic programs, research output, and global engagement. The university is committed to providing a high-quality education that prepares students for success in a rapidly changing world. It is also part of the Top Global University Project (SGU), emphasizing its dedication to internationalization and the development of globally competent graduates.

## üåç International Student Support

Meiji University provides a comprehensive range of support services for international students to ensure a smooth transition and a fulfilling academic experience. These services include:

- **Dormitory:** Meiji University offers on-campus and off-campus dormitory options for international students. These dormitories provide a safe and convenient living environment.
- **English Programs:** Many faculties offer courses taught in English, allowing international students to pursue their studies without Japanese language proficiency. The university also provides Japanese language courses to help students improve their language skills.
- **Career Support:** The university's career center offers career counseling, job search assistance, and internship opportunities to help international students achieve their career goals. They also host career fairs and workshops specifically tailored to international students.
- **Scholarships:** Meiji University offers a variety of scholarships for international students, based on academic merit and financial need. Information about scholarships is available on the university's website.
- **International Student Support Office:** The International Student Support Office provides a wide range of services, including visa assistance, orientation programs, and cultural events. This office serves as a central point of contact for international students and helps them navigate university life.
