## School Overview & Philosophy

Osaka High Technology College, located in Yodogawa-ku, Osaka City, is a vocational school that aims to cultivate practical, technology-focused human resources needed in today's rapidly changing society. The school goes beyond simply delivering theoretical knowledge and focuses on developing practical skills and problem-solving abilities that can be applied in real-world settings. Students gain in-depth learning experiences through various practical training environments and state-of-the-art facilities. The school provides a customized education system to maximize each student's potential and supports them in building successful careers in their chosen fields. It also actively operates foreign language education and international exchange programs to foster individuals with an international perspective.

Osaka High Technology College conducts education based on the core values of 'Creation,' 'Cooperation,' and 'Growth.' It helps students develop creative thinking skills to create new value and cultivates the ability to achieve common goals through teamwork and collaboration. Furthermore, it is dedicated to fostering individuals who can contribute to the future society through continuous self-development and growth. The school provides a comfortable and safe learning environment and supports students' academics and lives in various ways. Students can utilize the school's diverse support systems to move towards their dreams. The school will be a solid foundation for students' successful futures.

The school especially provides a systematic Japanese language education program for students aiming to advance to higher education. It spares no effort in providing comprehensive support necessary for EJU (Examination for Japanese University Admission for International Students) preparation, as well as admission to Japanese universities and graduate schools. Students can improve their Japanese language skills through customized classes for each level and receive practical help such as guidance on higher education and mock interviews. In addition, the school invites experts in various fields to hold special lectures and supports students in gaining broad knowledge and information. Osaka High Technology College is doing its best to help students successfully adapt to Japanese society and realize their dreams. All members of the school will continue to strive and develop for the successful future of students.
