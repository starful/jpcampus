Kobe Gaigo Kyoiku Gakuin, located in the vibrant and international port city of Kobe, is a Japanese language school dedicated to preparing students for higher education in Japan. With a large capacity and a clear focus on academic advancement, this institution is an excellent choice for students with serious goals of entering Japanese universities, graduate schools, or vocational colleges.

A key distinguishing feature of the school is its specialized support for students aiming for science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) fields, offering tailored preparation for the demanding science subjects on the Examination for Japanese University Admission (EJU).
