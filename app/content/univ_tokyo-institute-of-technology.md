## üåç International Student Support

Tokyo Tech provides extensive support for international students, including:

*   **Dormitory:** On-campus and off-campus housing options are available for international students.  The university assists with finding suitable accommodation.
*   **English Programs:** Many courses and programs are offered in English, particularly at the graduate level. Japanese language courses are also available.
*   **Career Support:** The university provides career counseling and job placement assistance specifically tailored to international students. This includes workshops on resume writing, interview skills, and job search strategies in Japan.
*   **Scholarships:** A variety of scholarships are available to international students from organizations such as JASSO (Japan Student Services Organization) and the Japanese government (MEXT).
*   **Visa Support:** The international student office provides assistance with visa applications and renewals.
*   **Buddy System:** A buddy system pairs new international students with current students to help them adjust to life in Japan.
