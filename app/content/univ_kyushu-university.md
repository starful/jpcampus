## üåç International Student Support

Kyushu University is dedicated to providing comprehensive support to its international students, helping them adjust to life in Japan and succeed in their academic pursuits. The university offers various resources and services, including:

*   **Dormitory Accommodation:** Kyushu University provides on-campus and off-campus dormitory options for international students. These dormitories offer affordable and convenient housing, allowing students to focus on their studies and connect with other students.
*   **English-Taught Programs:** The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs taught entirely in English, catering to students from diverse academic backgrounds.
*   **Japanese Language Support:** International students can enroll in Japanese language courses to improve their communication skills and enhance their understanding of Japanese culture.
*   **Career Support:** The university's career services provide guidance and resources to help international students find internships and employment opportunities in Japan and abroad. They offer workshops on resume writing, interview skills, and job searching strategies.
*   **International Student Support Office:** A dedicated International Student Support Office provides assistance with visa applications, housing arrangements, and other practical matters. They also organize social and cultural events to help international students integrate into the university community.
*   **Scholarships:** A variety of scholarships are available to international students based on academic merit and financial need. These scholarships can help alleviate the financial burden of studying abroad.
