## üè´ University Overview

Kyushu University, abbreviated as Kyudai (‰πùÂ§ß), is a leading national university located in Fukuoka, Kyushu, Japan. It is one of Japan's prestigious Seven Imperial Universities, renowned for its high standards in education and research. Founded in 1911 as Kyushu Imperial University, it has a long and rich history of contributing significantly to various fields of study. Kyudai prides itself on its strong emphasis on both fundamental research and the application of knowledge to solve real-world problems. The university consistently ranks among the top universities in Japan and Asia.

Kyushu University is committed to fostering global leaders and promoting international collaborations. It actively encourages international students to join its diverse academic community and provides various support systems to ensure their success. The university has been selected as a Top Global University by the Japanese government, further strengthening its commitment to internationalization. It also boasts a comprehensive range of graduate programs, many of which are taught in English.

The university's reputation for excellence is reflected in the accomplishments of its faculty and alumni, who have made significant contributions to science, technology, business, and the arts. Kyudai has a strong focus on research and innovation, housing numerous research centers and institutes dedicated to cutting-edge investigations in various disciplines. It is particularly well-known for its research in areas such as engineering, medicine, and agriculture.

## üåç International Student Support

Kyushu University provides comprehensive support services for international students to ensure a smooth transition and a successful academic experience.

*   **Dormitory:** The university offers on-campus and off-campus housing options for international students, including dormitories and apartments. Accommodation is typically prioritized for newly arrived international students.
*   **English Programs:** Many graduate programs are offered in English, catering to international students who may not have proficiency in Japanese. Additionally, the university provides Japanese language courses for international students to improve their language skills.
*   **Career Support:** The university's career center provides career counseling, job search assistance, and internship opportunities to help international students find employment after graduation.
*   **International Student Support Office:** The International Student Support Office offers a range of services, including visa assistance, cultural orientation, and academic advising.
*   **Scholarships:** A variety of scholarships are available to international students, including scholarships from the Japanese government (MEXT), private foundations, and Kyushu University itself.
