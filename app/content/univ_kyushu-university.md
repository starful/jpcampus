## üåç International Student Support

Kyushu University provides comprehensive support for international students to ensure a smooth transition and enriching academic experience.

**Dormitory:**

The university offers on-campus and off-campus dormitory options for international students. The International Residence provides a comfortable and convenient living environment, fostering cultural exchange and community building.

**English Programs:**

Kyushu University offers various degree programs and courses taught in English, catering to international students who may not have proficiency in Japanese. These programs cover diverse fields, including engineering, science, and humanities. The university also provides Japanese language courses to help international students improve their language skills and integrate into Japanese society.

**Career Support:**

The university's Career and Scholarship Support Office offers career counseling, job search assistance, and internship opportunities for international students. They organize career fairs, workshops, and seminars to help students develop their professional skills and explore career options in Japan and abroad.

**Scholarships:**
Kyushu University offers a range of scholarships for international students, including those provided by the Japanese government (MEXT), private foundations, and the university itself. Information about available scholarships and application procedures can be found on the university's website.

## üìç Campus Location & Access

Kyushu University has several campuses, but the main campus is located in Hakozaki, Fukuoka.

| Campus         | Address                              | Access                                                                     |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hakozaki       | 6-10-1 Hakozaki, Higashi-ku, Fukuoka | A few minutes' walk from Hakozaki-kyudai-mae Station (Fukuoka City Subway) |
| Ito            | Motooka, Nishi-ku, Fukuoka           | Take a bus from Meinohama Station (Fukuoka City Subway)                    |
| Medical Campus | 3-1-1 Maidashi, Higashi-ku, Fukuoka  | 5 minutes walk from Maidashi-Kyudaibyoenmae subway station                 |

Access to the main Hakozaki campus is convenient via the Fukuoka City Subway. From Hakata Station, take the subway to Hakozaki-kyudai-mae Station and walk for a few minutes to reach the campus. The Ito campus, which is newer and larger, can be accessed by taking a bus from Meinohama Station on the Fukuoka City Subway. The Medical Campus is conveniently accessible from the Maidashi-Kyudaibyoenmae subway station.
