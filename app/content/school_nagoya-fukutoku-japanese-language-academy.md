The curriculum focuses on developing well-rounded language skills, including reading, writing, listening, and speaking. The school utilizes ability-based class placement to ensure that students are learning at an appropriate pace and level. Regular assessments are conducted to track student progress and make necessary adjustments to their learning plans.
