## üè´ University Overview

Toyo University, established in 1887 by Inoue Enryo, a philosopher, is a private university located in Tokyo, Japan. Initially founded as a school of philosophy, Toyo University has grown into a comprehensive institution offering a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs. The university is known for its strong emphasis on global studies, international exchange programs, and its commitment to fostering independent thinking and problem-solving skills in its students. Toyo University consistently ranks among the top universities in Japan and is recognized for its research contributions across various fields.

Toyo University has several campuses located primarily in the Tokyo metropolitan area. The main campus is located in Hakusan, Tokyo, but other campuses exist such as Asaka, Kawagoe, and Akabane. Each campus has its own distinct atmosphere and caters to specific faculties and departments. The university actively promotes international collaboration and has partnerships with numerous universities worldwide. This enables students and faculty to engage in exchange programs, joint research projects, and international conferences.

Toyo University is also a part of the Top Global University Project launched by the Japanese Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology (MEXT). This project aims to enhance the international competitiveness of Japanese universities. Toyo University has leveraged this initiative to further expand its international programs, attract more international students, and strengthen its research capabilities.

The university takes pride in its diverse student body and offers various support services for international students, including language assistance, cultural exchange events, and career counseling. Toyo University also has a well-established alumni network that provides valuable connections and opportunities for graduates.

## üåç International Student Support

Toyo University provides extensive support for international students to ensure a smooth transition and a fulfilling academic experience. Support services include:

- **Dormitory Accommodation:** The university offers dormitory facilities for international students, providing a convenient and affordable housing option. There are on-campus and off-campus options available, with varying amenities and costs.
- **English Programs:** Toyo University offers a variety of English-taught programs at both the undergraduate and graduate levels. These programs cater to international students who may not have sufficient Japanese language proficiency. They offer English language courses to support students' academic progress.
- **Japanese Language Support:** Japanese language courses are available for international students to improve their communication skills and integrate into Japanese society.
- **Career Support:** The university provides career counseling and job placement assistance for international students, helping them find internships and employment opportunities in Japan or abroad.
- **Visa Support:** The international student office assists with visa application and renewal procedures.
- **Cultural Exchange Events:** Toyo University organizes cultural exchange events to promote intercultural understanding and interaction between international and Japanese students.
