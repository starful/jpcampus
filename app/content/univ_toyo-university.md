## University Overview

Toyo University, established in 1887 by the philosopher Inoue Enryo, is a private university located in Tokyo, Japan. Originally founded as a school of philosophy, Toyo University has evolved into a comprehensive institution offering a diverse array of undergraduate and graduate programs. The university is recognized for its strong focus on global studies, international exchange programs, and its dedication to nurturing independent thinking and problem-solving skills among its students. Toyo University consistently ranks among the top universities in Japan and is acclaimed for its research contributions across various fields.

Toyo University operates several campuses primarily within the Tokyo metropolitan area. The main campus is situated in Hakusan, Tokyo, with other campuses located in Asaka, Kawagoe, and Akabane. Each campus possesses its own unique atmosphere and caters to specific faculties and departments. The university actively encourages international collaboration and maintains partnerships with numerous universities worldwide, facilitating exchange programs, joint research projects, and international conferences for students and faculty.

Toyo University is also a participant in the Top Global University Project, an initiative launched by the Japanese Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology (MEXT). This project is designed to enhance the international competitiveness of Japanese universities. Toyo University has capitalized on this initiative to further expand its international programs, attract a greater number of international students, and strengthen its research capabilities.

The university prides itself on its diverse student body and offers a range of support services for international students, including language assistance, cultural exchange events, and career counseling. Furthermore, Toyo University boasts a well-established alumni network that provides valuable connections and opportunities for graduates.

## International Student Support

Toyo University offers comprehensive support for international students to ensure a seamless transition and a rewarding academic journey. Support services include:

*   **Dormitory Accommodation:** The university provides dormitory facilities for international students, offering a convenient and affordable housing option. Both on-campus and off-campus options are available, with varying amenities and costs.
*   **English Programs:** Toyo University offers a range of English-taught programs at both the undergraduate and graduate levels. These programs are designed for international students who may not have sufficient Japanese language proficiency. The university also provides English language courses to support students' academic progress.
*   **Japanese Language Support:** Japanese language courses are available for international students to enhance their communication skills and integrate into Japanese society.
*   **Career Support:** The university offers career counseling and job placement assistance for international students, helping them to secure internships and employment opportunities in Japan or abroad.
*   **Visa Support:** The international student office provides assistance with visa application and renewal procedures.
*   **Cultural Exchange Events:** Toyo University organizes cultural exchange events to foster intercultural understanding and interaction between international and Japanese students.
