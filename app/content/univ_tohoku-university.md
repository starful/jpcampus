## University Overview

Tohoku University, situated in Sendai, Miyagi Prefecture, stands as a leading national university in Japan, celebrated for its exceptional research and dedication to innovation. Established in 1907 as Tohoku Imperial University, it was the third Imperial University founded in Japan and the first to welcome female students. This forward-thinking approach to inclusivity has been a defining feature of the university throughout its history. Tohoku University consistently ranks among the top universities both in Japan and worldwide, particularly recognized for its strengths in materials science, engineering, and physics.

The university has been instrumental in the advancement of numerous technologies in Japan, making significant contributions to areas like semiconductors, magnetic materials, and robotics. The principles of 'Practical Research' and 'Open Doors' are deeply embedded in the university's academic atmosphere, encouraging researchers to pursue cutting-edge research addressing real-world issues and fostering collaboration across disciplines and with international partners. Tohoku University is also designated as a 'Top Global University' by the Japanese government, underscoring its commitment to internationalization and its efforts to attract talented students and researchers globally. The university maintains a strong connection with the local community, contributing to the economic and cultural development of the Tohoku region.

## Faculties & Departments

Tohoku University offers a broad spectrum of undergraduate and graduate programs through its various faculties and graduate schools. The university is well-regarded for its strong focus on graduate-level education and research.

*   Faculty of Arts and Letters: Philosophy, History, Japanese Language and Literature, Western Languages and Literature, Behavioral Sciences
*   Faculty of Education: Education, Educational Psychology, Special Needs Education
*   Faculty of Law: Law
*   Faculty of Economics: Economics, Business Administration
*   Faculty of Science: Mathematics, Physics, Astronomy, Geophysics, Chemistry, Biology
*   School of Medicine: Medicine
*   School of Dentistry: Dentistry
*   Faculty of Pharmaceutical Sciences: Pharmaceutical Sciences
*   Faculty of Engineering: Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Materials Science and Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Architecture, Information Science and Engineering, Aerospace Engineering
*   Faculty of Agriculture: Agronomy, Agricultural Chemistry, Forest Science, Animal Science, Fisheries Science

## Tuition & Fees

The tuition fees at Tohoku University are standard for national universities in Japan. International students generally pay the same fees as domestic students. Scholarship opportunities are available to help offset the costs. The amounts listed below are approximate and subject to change.

*   Admission Fee: 282,000 JPY
*   Yearly Tuition: 535,800 JPY

## International Student Support

Tohoku University provides extensive support for international students to facilitate a smooth transition and a rewarding academic experience. This includes assistance with visa applications, housing, and daily life in Japan.

*   **Dormitory:** The university provides several dormitories for international students, offering affordable and convenient housing options. Application processes vary, so it's recommended to apply early.
*   **English Programs:** While some programs are taught entirely in Japanese, Tohoku University offers an increasing number of programs conducted in English, particularly at the graduate level. The Future Global Leadership (FGL) program is a prime example, offering several undergraduate and graduate programs in English across various fields.
*   **Career Support:** The university's Career Support Center offers guidance and resources to assist international students in finding internships and employment opportunities both in Japan and abroad. They provide workshops on resume writing, interview skills, and job search strategies.
*   **Japanese Language Classes:** Tohoku University provides Japanese language courses for international students at various levels, helping them improve their language proficiency and integrate into Japanese society.
*   **International Student Support Office:** The International Student Support Office offers assistance with a wide range of issues, including academic advising, personal counseling, and cultural exchange activities.

## Campus Location & Access

Tohoku University has several campuses located in Sendai City. The main campuses are:

*   **Katahira Campus:** This is the main administrative campus and houses many research institutes.
*   **Aobayama Campus:** Situated on a hill overlooking the city, this campus houses the Faculty of Science, Faculty of Engineering, and other research facilities.
*   **Kawauchi Campus:** Home to the Faculty of Arts and Letters, Faculty of Education, Faculty of Law, Faculty of Economics, and the undergraduate liberal arts program.
*   **Seiryo Campus:** The location of the School of Medicine, School of Dentistry, and related facilities.

*   Katahira Campus: JR Sendai Station, Subway Tozai Line (Aobadori Station). Approximately a 15-minute walk from JR Sendai Station or a short walk from Aobadori Station.
*   Aobayama Campus: Subway Tozai Line (Aobayama Station). Directly accessible from Aobayama Station.
*   Kawauchi Campus: Subway Tozai Line (Kawauchi Station). Directly accessible from Kawauchi Station.
*   Seiryo Campus: Subway Tozai Line (Itsutsubashi Station). Take the Tozai Line from Sendai Station to Itsutsubashi Station. It's a 5-minute walk from the station.
