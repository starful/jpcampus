Seijo University may be small, but its international outlook is robust. The International Center acts as a dedicated hub for foreign students, offering assistance with everything from visa renewals and housing to scholarship applications. 

### Language Support and Exchange
Because the university is not overwhelmed by thousands of international students, the ones who are there receive very focused Japanese language support. The Seijo-Gakuen-mae area is legendary in Tokyo for its greenery, quiet streets, and high-end residences. The campus itself is integrated into this neighborhood. The 'Ginkgo Trees' that line the paths become a beautiful gold in autumn, creating a serene environment for study. Despite the quiet surroundings, the university is only a 15-minute train ride from Shinjuku, providing easy access to the excitement of central Tokyo.
