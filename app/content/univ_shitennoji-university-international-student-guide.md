## 🏫 University Overview

Shitennoji University, often affectionately referred to as **IBU** (International Buddhist University), is a distinguished private institution located in Habikino City, Osaka. Founded on the profound spiritual and philosophical heritage of **Prince Shotoku**, one of Japan's most revered historical figures, the university stands as a modern academic manifestation of the 'Wa' (harmony) spirit. With a history that traces its roots back to the establishment of Shitennoji Temple over 1,400 years ago, the university offers a unique blend of traditional Japanese values and contemporary academic excellence.

For international students, Shitennoji University represents more than just a place of study; it is a community that emphasizes human connection, ethical grounding, and the development of a 'compassionate heart.' While it maintains its Buddhist identity, the university is deeply inclusive, welcoming students from all religious and cultural backgrounds to participate in its diverse undergraduate and graduate programs.

## 📜 The Philosophy of 'Wa' (Harmony)

The core identity of Shitennoji University is built upon the **Seventeen-Article Constitution** of Prince Shotoku, specifically the first article: 'Harmony is to be valued.' This philosophy permeates the campus culture, teaching methods, and student support services. For an international student, this translates into a highly supportive, non-competitive environment where faculty members take a personal interest in student welfare. The university aims to cultivate individuals who can contribute to global society by balancing professional expertise with a deep sense of social responsibility.

## 🎓 Academic Departments and Faculties

Shitennoji University is organized into several key faculties, each offering specialized tracks designed to meet the demands of the modern job market in Japan and abroad.

### 1. Faculty of Humanities
This faculty is the heart of cultural and linguistic studies at IBU. It includes departments dedicated to **Japanese Language and Literature**, **English Communication**, and **International Career** studies. International students often find a home here, especially those looking to master the Japanese language while exploring the depths of Japanese history and society. The 'International Career' track is specifically designed to bridge the gap between academic study and the global business environment.

### 2. Faculty of Education
Shitennoji is widely recognized in the Kansai region as a powerhouse for teacher training. The university has a long-standing reputation for producing high-quality educators for elementary schools, junior highs, and high schools across Japan. For international students interested in pedagogy or the Japanese education system, this faculty offers rigorous training and access to a massive network of alumni working in the education sector.

### 3. Faculty of Management
Focusing on the practical aspects of business, the Faculty of Management offers courses in **Business Administration** and **Public Management**. The curriculum emphasizes 'hands-on' learning, encouraging students to participate in local revitalization projects and internships. For international students, the focus on Japanese business etiquette and management styles is invaluable for future employment within Japan.

### 4. Faculty of Nursing
Established to meet the growing demand for healthcare professionals in an aging society, this faculty provides state-of-the-art facilities. While the language barrier for nursing is high, the program is noted for its focus on 'human-centric care,' aligning with the university’s Buddhist principles.

## 🌏 International Student Support and Global Center

The **Global Education Center** at Shitennoji University serves as the primary hub for international students. Recognizing that moving to a new country is a significant challenge, the university provides comprehensive support systems:

- **Japanese Language Support:** Beyond standard classes, there are specialized Japanese language programs tailored to different proficiency levels, ensuring students can keep up with their specialized subjects.
- **The Tutor System:** International students are often paired with Japanese 'student tutors' who help them navigate daily life in Japan, from opening a bank account to understanding local social norms. This system fosters deep cross-cultural friendships.
- **Scholarships:** IBU offers several internal scholarships specifically for privately financed international students, often providing tuition reductions of 30% to 50% based on academic performance and financial need.
- **Cultural Excursions:** The university organizes regular trips to historical sites in Osaka, Nara, and Kyoto, allowing students to experience the 'real' Japan beyond the classroom.

## 🏢 Campus Life and Facilities

The campus in Habikino is a beautiful blend of greenery and modern architecture. It provides a peaceful atmosphere conducive to study, away from the frantic pace of central Osaka, yet close enough to remain connected. 

- **The Library:** A massive repository of knowledge with a significant collection of Buddhist texts, as well as a wide range of international academic journals and digital resources.
- **Cafeteria and Social Spaces:** The university cafeterias offer affordable, nutritious meals, including options that cater to various dietary preferences. The 'Global Lounge' is a popular spot where international and local students mingle to practice languages.
- **Clubs and Circles:** With over 50 student organizations, ranging from traditional Japanese arts (like Tea Ceremony and Kendo) to modern sports and music, international students are encouraged to join these groups to integrate into the student body fully.

## 💼 Career Prospects and Employment

One of the strongest selling points of Shitennoji University is its **Career Support Center**. The university boasts an exceptionally high employment rate for its graduates (often exceeding 98%). For international students wishing to remain in Japan, the center offers:

- **Individual Career Counseling:** One-on-one sessions to help students identify their strengths and find suitable companies.
- **Job Hunting Seminars:** Specialized workshops on how to write a Japanese-style resume (Rirekisho) and how to navigate the complex 'Shukatsu' (job hunting) season.
- **Company Information Sessions:** Many local and regional companies visit the campus specifically to recruit Shitennoji students, valuing their reputation for being hard-working and ethically minded.

## 📍 Living in Habikino, Osaka

Habikino City is a unique location. It is famous for the **Mozu-Furuichi Kofun Group**, ancient burial mounds that are a UNESCO World Heritage site. Living here offers a more affordable lifestyle compared to central Osaka or Tokyo. 

- **Cost of Living:** Rent for student-friendly apartments in Habikino is significantly lower than in Namba or Umeda. 
- **Access:** The university is accessible via the Kintetsu Minami-Osaka Line. A short train ride brings you to **Abeno Harukas** (the tallest skyscraper in Japan) and the vibrant Tennoji district, which is a major transportation hub in Osaka.
- **Community:** The local community is welcoming, and students often find part-time jobs (arubaito) in local shops and restaurants, providing further opportunities to practice Japanese.

## 💡 Why Choose Shitennoji University?

For an international student, Shitennoji University offers a 'boutique' educational experience. Unlike massive national universities where one might feel like just another number, IBU provides a 'human-scale' education. It is an ideal choice for students who:

1.  **Seek a deep connection with Japanese culture:** The Buddhist heritage and emphasis on 'Wa' provide a cultural immersion that is hard to find elsewhere.
2.  **Want to become teachers or managers:** The specialized training in Education and Management is highly respected in the Kansai region.
3.  **Value a supportive environment:** The combination of the tutor system and the Global Education Center ensures that no student is left to struggle alone.

In conclusion, Shitennoji University is a sanctuary of learning that prepares students not just for a career, but for a meaningful life. It bridges the ancient wisdom of Prince Shotoku with the skills required for the 21st century, making it a hidden gem for international students in Japan.
