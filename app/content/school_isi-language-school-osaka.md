## üè´ School Overview and Philosophy

ISI Language School Osaka brings together students from various countries, aiming to improve not only their Japanese language skills but also to deepen their understanding of Japanese culture. We respect each student's learning goals and provide optimized educational programs to meet various needs, including further education, employment, and cultural experiences. In particular, we operate EJU (Examination for Japanese University Admission for International Students) preparation classes for students aiming to enter universities, and we are characterized by supporting in-depth learning in specific fields such as science, art, and design. The school spares no effort in providing meticulous counseling and support for students' successful study abroad life in Japan and strives to develop global human resources with an international sense.

Based on a student-centered educational philosophy, ISI Language School Osaka focuses not only on teaching Japanese but also on helping students successfully adapt to Japanese society and plan for the future. Experienced instructors constantly check students' learning progress and understanding and provide individual feedback to maximize learning effectiveness. In addition, through various cultural experience activities and exchange programs, we support students to deepen their understanding of Japanese culture and naturally improve their Japanese language skills through interaction with Japanese students. These efforts contribute to helping students grow with pride as members of Japanese society.

ISI Language School Osaka strives to provide the optimal learning environment. Classrooms and study spaces equipped with the latest facilities help students concentrate on their studies comfortably, and we provide various learning materials and books to improve students' self-directed learning abilities. In addition, the school is conveniently located in the center of Osaka, with easy access to transportation, and is surrounded by various convenience facilities and cultural facilities, allowing students to enjoy a fulfilling study abroad life. ISI Language School Osaka will be a reliable partner that helps students realize their dreams and plan for the future, going beyond being just a language school.

ISI Language School Osaka offers a wide range of programs, from short-term courses to long-term higher education courses, to meet the diverse needs of students. In particular, short-term courses are popular among students who want to experience Japanese culture along with Japanese language learning, and long-term higher education courses provide systematic learning curriculums and guidance for higher education for students aiming to enter universities. In addition, the Business Japanese course provides practical help for students preparing for employment, and the Art and Design course provides students who want to enter related fields with the opportunity to acquire specialized knowledge and skills. ISI Language School Osaka actively supports students in choosing the optimal learning path according to their goals.

## üè° Accommodation & Living Support

ISI Language School Osaka offers a variety of accommodation options to ensure students can live comfortably and safely. The most common form of accommodation is a dormitory, which is located close to the school for easy commuting. Homestays are a great opportunity to live in a Japanese home, experience Japanese culture directly, and improve your Japanese language skills. Share houses are an opportunity to live and interact with other students, and apartments are suitable for students who want an independent life. ISI Language School Osaka provides a variety of information to help students choose the best accommodation to suit their preferences and budget, and supports accommodation contracts and move-in procedures.

ISI Language School Osaka provides a variety of support to help students adapt to life in Japan. Student counselors listen to students' concerns and provide necessary information to help students have a stable study abroad life. ISI Language School Osaka spares no effort in providing meticulous consideration so that students do not experience difficulties due to language barriers and cultural differences.
