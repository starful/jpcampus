## University Overview

Senshu University (専修大学, Senshu Daigaku) is a well-regarded private university located in Tokyo and Kanagawa, Japan. Established in 1880 as Senshu Gakko by a group of leading legal scholars and economists, it is one of Japan's oldest private higher education institutions. It was founded with the goal of promoting the study of modern law and economics, which were essential for Japan's modernization during the Meiji era. The university officially adopted its current name in 1949.

Throughout its history, Senshu University has maintained a strong tradition of practical education and a commitment to developing individuals with a broad perspective and ethical foundation. Its educational philosophy emphasizes critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a global perspective, preparing students for leadership roles in various sectors of society. Senshu University is particularly known for its programs in law, economics, and business administration, which consistently attract a large number of applicants. It plays a significant role in the Tokyo private university landscape and is often considered one of the prominent institutions in the Kanto region, providing a solid academic foundation. While not part of the elite 'MARCH' group, it remains highly respected and competitive.

With two main campuses – the green Ikuta Campus in Kawasaki, Kanagawa Prefecture, and the urban Kanda Campus in central Tokyo – Senshu University provides diverse learning environments. The Ikuta Campus serves as the primary hub for most undergraduate faculties, offering a spacious and modern academic setting with advanced facilities for research and student activities. The Kanda Campus, located in the heart of Tokyo's academic and publishing district, primarily focuses on evening courses, graduate programs, and professional development, leveraging its downtown location for greater engagement with industry and culture. The university is dedicated to fostering an inclusive academic community, embracing both domestic and international students in its pursuit of academic excellence and societal contribution, upholding its founding spirit of cultivating professionals who can contribute to a dynamic global society.

## Faculties & Departments

Senshu University offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate programs across eight faculties, providing diverse fields of study tailored to meet the evolving demands of society. Each faculty is committed to delivering high-quality education and research, preparing students for professional careers and further academic pursuits.

*   **School of Economics:** Offers programs in economics theory, policy, and data analysis. Focuses on understanding global economic trends, market mechanisms, and socio-economic issues, with an emphasis on practical application and research.
*   **School of Law:** Provides a robust legal education, covering constitutional law, civil law, criminal law, and international law. Emphasizes critical legal thinking, jurisprudence, and practical application for aspiring legal professionals and public servants.
*   **School of Business Administration:** Concentrates on modern business practices, management strategies, marketing, and accounting. Prepares students for leadership roles in corporations, non-profits, and entrepreneurial ventures through case studies and practical projects.
*   **School of Humanities:** Explores diverse aspects of human culture, history, philosophy, literature, and psychology. Fosters interdisciplinary understanding, critical analysis of human experience, and a deep appreciation for cultural diversity.
*   **School of Commerce:** Focuses on the principles and practices of commerce, including trade, finance, marketing, and business logistics. Aims to cultivate professionals capable of navigating complex commercial environments, with a strong foundation in business ethics and global perspectives.
*   **School of Network and Information:** Combines aspects of information science, computer science, and network engineering. Develops skills in IT, data management, cybersecurity, and digital communication, preparing students for the rapidly evolving tech industry and digital society.
*   **School of International Cultural Relations:** Offers studies in international relations, global cultures, languages, and cross-cultural communication. Prepares students for careers in international organizations, diplomacy, global business, and cultural exchange, fostering intercultural competence.
*   **School of Human Development:** Focuses on human growth, education, welfare, and community development. Addresses contemporary social issues and aims to cultivate professionals who can contribute to individual well-being and societal progress, emphasizing practical skills in fields like social work and education.

## International Student Support

Senshu University is committed to providing a supportive and enriching environment for its international students, facilitating their academic success and daily life in Japan. The university offers a range of services designed to help international students integrate smoothly into university life and Japanese society.

*   **Dormitory Accommodation:** Senshu University operates the 'Senshu International House,' a dedicated dormitory for international students. This facility offers convenient and affordable living options, fostering a sense of community among students from diverse cultural backgrounds. While it provides a safe and comfortable environment, spaces can be competitive, and early application is highly recommended.
*   **English-Taught Programs:** While the vast majority of undergraduate degree programs at Senshu University are taught in Japanese, requiring a strong command of the language, the university does offer some courses taught in English, primarily for exchange students or as part of specific international programs. Prospective full-degree international students typically need to demonstrate proficiency in Japanese (e.g., through the EJU – Examination for Japanese University Admission for International Students – and the Japanese Language Proficiency Test – JLPT).
*   **Scholarships:** Senshu University offers various scholarship opportunities specifically for international students, available both upon admission and during their studies. These include the 'Senshu University Scholarship for International Students,' as well as guidance and support for applying to external scholarships provided by the Japanese government (MEXT) and numerous private foundations. Eligibility criteria for these scholarships typically depend on academic performance, financial need, and other specific requirements.
*   **Career Support:** The university's Career Center provides comprehensive support to all students, including international students, to help them navigate the competitive Japanese job market. This includes workshops on resume writing (Japanese-style), interview preparation, hosting job fairs, and offering individual counseling sessions. Specialized guidance tailored to the unique needs and challenges of international students seeking employment in Japan or abroad is also available, assisting with cultural nuances and employment regulations.
*   **International Student Office:** The International Student Office serves as a central point of contact for international students, offering a wide array of support services. This includes assistance with visa procedures, housing arrangements, daily life advice, cultural adjustment, and academic counseling. The office also organizes orientation programs for newcomers and various cultural exchange events throughout the year to help international students integrate into the university community and experience Japanese culture.

## Campus Location & Access

Senshu University operates two primary campuses, each offering distinct advantages and access points within the Tokyo metropolitan area and its surroundings. The two campuses provide different atmospheres, catering to a diverse student body and a wide range of academic pursuits.

### Ikuta Campus (Main Campus)

Located in Kawasaki, Kanagawa Prefecture, the Ikuta Campus is the largest and primary campus, housing most undergraduate faculties. It offers a spacious, green environment conducive to learning and student life, with modern academic buildings, sports facilities, and extensive libraries. It's known for its serene setting away from the intense urban bustle.
