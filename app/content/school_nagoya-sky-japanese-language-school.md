Teaching style emphasizes interactive learning, with group activities, discussions, and presentations designed to foster communication skills and critical thinking.

**1-Year 9-Month Prep Course:** This accelerated program is designed for students who want to enter university as quickly as possible. The teaching style is more personalized, with instructors providing individual feedback and guidance to each student.

**1-Year 3-Month Prep Course:** This program is designed for students with a strong foundation in Japanese who want to further refine their skills and prepare for the EJU. The teaching style is challenging and stimulating, encouraging students to think critically and creatively.

**Short-Term Course:** For students who want to experience learning Japanese in Japan without committing to a long-term program, Nagoya SKY offers short-term courses.
