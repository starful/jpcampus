## üåç International Student Support

Tokyo University of Science is committed to providing comprehensive support for international students. This includes:

- **Dormitory:** TUS offers dormitory accommodations for international students, providing a convenient and affordable housing option.
- **English Programs:** While the primary language of instruction is Japanese, some courses, particularly at the graduate level, are offered in English.
- **Career Support:** The university provides career counseling and job placement assistance to help international students find employment opportunities after graduation.
- **Japanese Language Support**: Japanese language courses are available for international students to help them improve their communication skills.
- **Visa Support:** The university assists international students with the visa application process.
