## üè´ University Overview

Kobe University, established in 1902 as Kobe Higher Commercial School, is a leading national university located in Kobe, Japan. It is renowned for its strong programs in economics, business administration, and law, and it has consistently ranked among the top universities in Japan. Kobe University is part of the prestigious National Seven Universities of Japan. The university's mission is to contribute to society through the creation and dissemination of knowledge, fostering global citizenship, and promoting collaboration across disciplines.

Kobe University has a long history of international collaboration and welcomes students from all over the world. The university's commitment to internationalization is reflected in its numerous exchange programs, joint research projects, and English-taught degree programs. It also has a beautiful campus overlooking the city of Kobe and the Seto Inland Sea, providing a stimulating and conducive learning environment.

## üåç International Student Support

Kobe University provides comprehensive support for international students, including:

*   **Dormitory:** The university offers on-campus and off-campus housing options for international students. International Residence Hall provides comfortable and affordable accommodation.
*   **English Programs:** A range of degree programs and courses are taught in English, catering to students from diverse backgrounds. Japanese language courses are also available.
*   **Career Support:** The university provides career counseling, job search assistance, and internship opportunities for international students.
*   **Visa Support:** Assistance with visa application and renewal processes.
*   **Counseling Services:** Personal and academic counseling services are available to help students adjust to life in Japan.
*   **International Student Association:** The ISA organizes social events and activities to promote cultural exchange and build a sense of community among international students.
