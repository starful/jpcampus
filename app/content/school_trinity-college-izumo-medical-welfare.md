## School Overview

Trinity College Izumo Medical & Welfare Professional Training College (トリニティカレッジ出雲医療福祉専門学校) is a vocational school located in Izumo, Shimane Prefecture, Japan. While technically a vocational school specializing in medical and welfare fields, it also offers comprehensive Japanese language programs designed to prepare international students for higher education in Japan. The school's philosophy centers on fostering compassionate and skilled professionals ready to contribute to society. The campus atmosphere is described as supportive and encouraging, with a strong emphasis on individual attention and student well-being.

Located in Izumo City, Shimane Prefecture, the school offers a quieter, more traditional Japanese experience compared to larger metropolitan areas. ## Curriculum & Courses

Trinity College offers several Japanese language courses tailored to different proficiency levels and academic goals. The curriculum focuses on practical communication skills alongside grammar and reading comprehension, preparing students for both daily life and academic pursuits. EJU preparation is a core component, especially for those aiming to enter Japanese universities.

**Available Courses:**

*   **Caregiving Advancement Course (介護進学コース):** Designed for students who want to enter caregiving-related vocational schools or universities. Focuses on Japanese language skills necessary for that field.
*   **Japanese Language Advancement 2-Year Course (日本語進学2年コース):** An intensive two-year program for students with little to no prior Japanese knowledge aiming to enter universities or other higher education institutions.
*   **Japanese Language Advancement 1.5-Year Course (日本語進学1.5年コース):** A shorter, more accelerated program for students with some existing Japanese knowledge who wish to prepare for higher education.

**Curriculum Levels:**

*   **Beginner:** Basic grammar, vocabulary, and sentence structure. Introduction to Hiragana and Katakana. (JLPT Target: N5, Duration: 3-6 months approximate)
*   **Elementary:** Expanding vocabulary, understanding more complex grammar, and practicing conversational skills. (JLPT Target: N4, Duration: 3-6 months approximate)
*   **Intermediate:** Refining grammar, improving reading comprehension, and developing fluency in spoken Japanese. (JLPT Target: N3, Duration: 6-9 months approximate)
*   **Advanced:** Mastering complex grammar, enhancing reading and writing skills, and preparing for academic Japanese. (JLPT Target: N2/N1, Duration: 6-9 months approximate)

While the school's primary focus is on Japanese language proficiency for academic purposes, they likely incorporate elements of Japanese culture and potentially business Japanese to cater to diverse student needs. The availability of courses focused on Art and Design might relate to specific vocational tracks within the college, or could be elective options to broaden students' horizons.
