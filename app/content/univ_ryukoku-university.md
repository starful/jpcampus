## üè´ University Overview

Ryukoku University, established in 1639, boasts a rich history deeply intertwined with Jodo Shinshu Buddhism. Originating as a seminary for Buddhist studies, the university has evolved into a comprehensive institution offering a wide range of academic disciplines while maintaining its commitment to its founding principles. Ryukoku University is recognized for its strong academic programs, research contributions, and dedication to fostering global citizens.

The university has two main campuses: Fukakusa Campus and Omiya Campus. Fukakusa Campus primarily houses the faculties of Letters, Economics, Business Administration, Law, Science and Technology, Sociology, Policy Science, International Studies, and Agriculture. Omiya Campus, the original site of the university, is home to the Faculty of Intercultural Communication and serves as a historical and cultural center.

Ryukoku University is dedicated to providing a diverse and inclusive learning environment. It actively promotes international exchange programs and encourages students to develop a global perspective. The university's commitment to social responsibility is reflected in its various outreach programs and research initiatives that address societal challenges.

üåç International Student Support

Ryukoku University offers comprehensive support services for international students, including:

*   **Dormitory:** On-campus and off-campus housing options are available.
*   **English Programs:** A variety of courses are offered in English, particularly within the Faculty of International Studies and other departments. Japanese language courses are also provided to help international students improve their language skills.
*   **Career Support:** Career services are available to assist international students with job searching, resume writing, and interview skills.
*   **Scholarships:** A range of scholarships are offered to international students based on academic merit and financial need.
*   **Buddy System:** A buddy system connects international students with local students to provide support and friendship.
