The school aims to enhance students' Japanese language skills and equip them with the knowledge and cultural understanding necessary to succeed in their chosen fields within Japan.

This emphasis on community creates a welcoming and supportive atmosphere, making Toho International College an ideal starting point for international students beginning their academic journey in Japan.

A significant portion of the course is dedicated to preparing students for the EJU, covering subjects such as Japanese as a Foreign Language, Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), and Japan and the World. Teaching methods involve a combination of classroom instruction, interactive exercises, group projects, and multimedia resources. The school provides dedicated teachers experienced in teaching international students. Special attention is given to scientific subjects, with supplementary classes and tutorials offered to support students pursuing STEM fields.

**University Prep 1 Year 9 Months:** This accelerated program builds upon the foundation of the two-year course, compressing the curriculum into a shorter timeframe. Designed for students with some initial Japanese language exposure, this course intensifies language learning, accelerates EJU preparation, and reinforces core subject knowledge. Teaching styles incorporate more independent study, focused practice exercises, and advanced-level discussions. The goal is to prepare students to enter the university of their choice within the shorter amount of time.

**University Prep 1 Year 6 Months:** This intensive course is tailored for students who possess a good understanding of basic Japanese grammar and vocabulary. The curriculum focuses on refining language proficiency, mastering EJU content, and honing academic skills. Teaching methods emphasize practical application of knowledge through problem-solving, essay writing, and presentation skills. The school provides extensive study materials and mock exams to help students prepare for the EJU.

**University Prep 1 Year 3 Months:** Designed for students with strong intermediate-level Japanese language skills, this fast-paced program concentrates on advanced EJU preparation and university application strategies. The curriculum includes intensive practice exams, personalized feedback sessions, and targeted support for specific subject areas. Teaching methods promote independent learning, critical thinking, and effective time management.

**University Prep 1 Year:** This condensed one-year course is ideal for students who possess a solid foundation in Japanese and aim to quickly prepare for university entrance exams. The curriculum covers all essential aspects of the EJU, including Japanese as a Foreign Language, Mathematics, Science, and Japan and the World. **General Japanese 1 Year:** This course is designed for students who wish to improve their general Japanese language proficiency without necessarily pursuing university entrance. The curriculum is flexible and adaptable to the individual needs and goals of each student. This course is suitable for students who want to improve their communication skills, explore Japanese culture, or prepare for the JLPT.
