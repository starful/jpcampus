## üè´ University Overview

Sophia University (‰∏äÊô∫Â§ßÂ≠¶, J≈çchi Daigaku) is a private research university in Tokyo, Japan. It is one of the most prestigious private universities in Japan, consistently ranked among the top universities in the country. Founded in 1913 by the Society of Jesus (Jesuits), Sophia University embodies the Jesuit educational philosophy of 'cura personalis,' which emphasizes the holistic development of the individual. This approach fosters critical thinking, ethical leadership, and a commitment to service.

Sophia University's history is deeply intertwined with Japan's modernization and internationalization. It played a pivotal role in promoting intercultural understanding and fostering connections between Japan and the rest of the world. The university is known for its strong international outlook, attracting students and faculty from diverse backgrounds. It actively promotes global learning through exchange programs, international collaborations, and courses taught in English.

Sophia University is recognized as a Super Global University (SGU) by the Japanese government, a designation that reflects its commitment to becoming a globally competitive and internationally diverse institution. The SGU initiative provides funding and support for universities to enhance their international programs, attract more international students and faculty, and foster research collaborations with leading universities around the world. As an SGU university, Sophia has made significant strides in expanding its English-based degree programs and creating a more welcoming environment for international students. The university's location in the heart of Tokyo provides students with unparalleled access to cultural, social, and professional opportunities.

The university's main campus is located in Yotsuya, a vibrant area of Tokyo known for its blend of tradition and modernity. It is easily accessible by public transportation, making it convenient for students to commute from various parts of the city. The Yotsuya campus features a mix of modern and historic buildings, creating a unique and inspiring learning environment. Sophia University also has other campuses and facilities located throughout Tokyo, including the Ichigaya campus, which houses some of its graduate programs.

## üåç International Student Support

Sophia University is committed to providing comprehensive support to its international students. This includes:

*   **Dormitory Accommodation:** Sophia University offers on-campus and off-campus dormitory options for international students. These dormitories provide a safe and comfortable living environment and foster a sense of community among international students.
*   **English-Based Degree Programs:** A wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs are offered in English, catering to students who may not have a strong command of Japanese. These programs provide a global perspective and prepare students for international careers.
*   **Japanese Language Courses:** Sophia University offers Japanese language courses at various levels, from beginner to advanced. These courses help international students develop their language skills and integrate into Japanese society.
*   **Career Support Services:** The university's career services center provides guidance and support to international students in their job search. This includes resume writing workshops, interview preparation, and networking events.
*   **Student Clubs and Organizations:** Sophia University has a wide range of student clubs and organizations, providing opportunities for international students to connect with other students, pursue their interests, and experience Japanese culture.
*   **International Student Advisors:** Dedicated international student advisors are available to provide assistance with visa issues, housing, and other practical matters.
