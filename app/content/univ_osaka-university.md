## üåç International Student Support

Osaka University provides extensive support for international students to ensure a smooth and enriching academic experience. This includes:

*   **Dormitory:** The university offers a variety of accommodation options for international students, including on-campus dormitories and off-campus apartments. These facilities are designed to provide a comfortable and supportive living environment.
*   **English Programs:** Numerous degree programs and courses are offered in English, particularly at the graduate level, making it accessible for students who may not be fluent in Japanese. Intensive Japanese language courses are also available to help students improve their language skills.
*   **Career Support:** The university's career center provides guidance and resources to help international students find internships and employment opportunities in Japan and abroad. Workshops, seminars, and individual consultations are offered to assist students with resume writing, interview skills, and job searching.
*   **Student Support Services:** International student advisors are available to provide assistance with visa applications, housing, health insurance, and other administrative matters. Cultural exchange programs and social events are organized to help international students integrate into the university community and experience Japanese culture.
