## School Overview

Osafune Japanese Language Academy, located in Okayama Prefecture, offers a comprehensive Japanese language education for international students aiming to pursue further studies in Japan. The academy emphasizes holistic development, focusing not only on language proficiency but also on cultural understanding and skills necessary for success in Japanese universities and the professional world. With a capacity of 225 students, Osafune fosters a close-knit community where students receive personalized attention and support. The school prioritizes creating a friendly and encouraging atmosphere, making it easier for students to adapt to life in Japan and thrive academically.

## Curriculum & Courses

Osafune Japanese Language Academy offers a structured curriculum designed to accommodate students of various proficiency levels, from beginners to advanced learners. The courses are tailored to prepare students for the Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) and the Examination for Japanese University Admission for International Students (EJU).

The academy provides the following courses:

*   **Basic Course:** Designed for beginners, focusing on fundamental grammar, vocabulary, and basic conversational skills.
*   **Standard Course:** Builds upon the Basic Course, expanding grammar knowledge and improving reading, writing, and speaking abilities. Prepares students for JLPT N4 and N3 levels.
*   **Advanced Course:** Targets advanced learners aiming for JLPT N2 and N1 levels. Emphasizes complex grammar, academic writing, and advanced conversational skills. Prepares students for university entrance exams.

The curriculum is structured around the following levels:

*   Basic Course: Foundational Japanese Skills, 1.5 years (18 months), October Admission
*   Standard Course: Intermediate Japanese Skills and JLPT N4/N3 Preparation, 1.5 years (18 months), July Admission
*   Advanced Course: Advanced Japanese Skills and JLPT N2/N1/EJU Preparation, 2 years (24 months), April Admission

In addition to core Japanese language courses, Osafune also offers elective classes focusing on:

*   Business Japanese: Equips students with the language and communication skills necessary for working in a Japanese business environment.
*   EJU Preparation: Provides targeted instruction and practice for the EJU exam, covering subjects such as Japanese, mathematics, science, and general studies.
*   Conversational Japanese: Focuses on improving fluency and naturalness in spoken Japanese.
*   Japanese Culture: Introduces students to various aspects of Japanese culture, including traditions, customs, and social norms.
*   Art and Design Portfolio Preparation: Prepares students applying to art and design universities.
