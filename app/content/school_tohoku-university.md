## School Overview & Philosophy

Tohoku University is a prestigious national university located in Sendai, Miyagi Prefecture, Japan. Established in 1907, it has contributed to social development while pursuing academic excellence based on its founding principles of "Research First" and "Open Door." Notably, Tohoku University was the first university in Japan to open its doors to women, practicing the progressive value of providing educational opportunities to all people regardless of gender or nationality. Currently, as a world-class research-oriented university, it conducts innovative research in various fields and strives to cultivate global leaders.

Tohoku University has been selected for the SGU (Super Global University) project and is creating an international educational environment. It attracts talented individuals from around the world through degree programs conducted in English and various international exchange programs, and provides students with opportunities to enhance their global capabilities. In addition, Japanese and international students study and interact together, fostering an atmosphere of understanding and respect for each other's cultures and values. Through this, Tohoku University is creating a truly international campus.

Tohoku University offers a variety of academic fields in undergraduate and graduate programs and excels in science and engineering. World-class research is actively conducted in the Faculty of Engineering, Faculty of Science, Faculty of Medicine, and other faculties, and it has a track record of producing many Nobel Prize laureates. In addition, high-quality education is provided in the Faculty of Law, Faculty of Economics, Faculty of Arts and Letters, and other faculties in the humanities and social sciences, and it fosters talented individuals who will be active in various fields of society. Tohoku University provides a systematic education system and a rich research environment to maximize students' potential and supports them in acquiring the knowledge and skills necessary for a constantly changing society.

Tohoku University emphasizes a "practical learning spirit" and aims to develop individuals with practical abilities who can contribute to solving real-world social problems, as well as theoretical knowledge. Students gain on-site experience through various projects and internship programs and increase their understanding of society. In addition, they actively participate in activities that contribute to regional development through cooperation with the local community. Tohoku University educates students to have a sense of responsibility for society and to contribute to creating a better world by utilizing their knowledge and skills.

Tohoku University offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate courses, and each faculty and major operates a unique education program and curriculum. In particular, in the fields of science and engineering, it focuses on educating and researching cutting-edge technologies based on world-class research facilities and faculty. In the humanities and social sciences, various lectures, discussions, and research activities are provided to cultivate in-depth understanding and analytical skills for social issues. In addition, the university is increasing the proportion of English lectures and actively supporting exchange programs with overseas universities to foster students' international sense.

In addition, in experiment and practical training classes, students apply theoretical knowledge in practice and develop practical skills by directly experiencing the problem-solving process. ## Student Body & Campus Life

Tohoku University is an international university where students of various nationalities study and live together. In particular, international students from China, Korea, Taiwan, Southeast Asia, Europe, the United States, and other countries are actively interacting, and the campus is full of a multicultural atmosphere. As of 2023, approximately 2,000 international students are enrolled, accounting for approximately 10% of the total student population. One of the great advantages of Tohoku University is that students of various nationalities study and live together, understanding and respecting each other's cultures and values.

International students can use Tohoku University's various campus facilities. Various convenient facilities such as libraries, research facilities, sports facilities, and student restaurants are well equipped, and students can freely use them according to their needs. In addition, they can participate in various activities such as club activities, volunteer activities, and cultural events to enjoy campus life. In particular, various support programs are operated for international students, providing various services such as Japanese language learning support, life counseling, and cultural experience events. Through this, international students are supported to quickly adapt to life in Japan and have a successful university life.

Tohoku University's campus is surrounded by a beautiful natural environment. Sendai is a city with abundant greenery, and there are many parks and forests around the campus, providing students with many spaces to rest and recharge. In addition, Sendai is one of Japan's major cities and has various cultural facilities and shopping facilities, allowing students to enjoy a convenient life. Tohoku University is doing its best to ensure that students can concentrate on their studies in a comfortable and safe environment.

## Accommodation & Living Support

Tohoku University offers a variety of accommodation options for international students. Students can choose from various types of accommodation such as dormitories, apartments, and homestays, and can choose accommodation that suits their budget and preferences. Dormitories are facilities operated by the school and offer relatively low-cost living and opportunities to interact with other students. Apartments are suitable for students who want to secure personal space, and homestays help students experience Japanese culture and learn Japanese while living in a Japanese home.

Dormitories generally have basic living supplies such as furniture, bedding, refrigerators, and air conditioners, and have access to shared kitchens, laundry rooms, and shower rooms. Dormitory managers support students' lives and provide assistance in case of problems. Apartments require a security deposit and monthly rent, and furniture and living supplies must be purchased directly. Homestays provide meals and allow you to experience the daily life of a Japanese family. Tohoku University offers various accommodation options to ensure that students can live safely and comfortably, and provides information on accommodation options.

Tohoku University provides various living support services in addition to accommodation. It provides various services such as Japanese language learning support, medical support, insurance enrollment support, and visa renewal support to help students quickly adapt to life in Japan. In addition, the International Student Counseling Center provides counseling on the difficulties students face in Japanese life and provides necessary information. Tohoku University provides a variety of support throughout life so that students can concentrate on their studies.
