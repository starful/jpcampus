## üåç International Student Support

The University of Tokyo provides extensive support for international students to ensure a smooth and enriching academic experience. This includes:

- **Dormitory:** Todai offers various accommodation options for international students, including university dormitories and off-campus housing. These residences provide a safe and comfortable living environment.
