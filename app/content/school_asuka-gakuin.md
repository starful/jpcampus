## School Overview

Asuka Academy, located in Yokohama, is a Japanese language school dedicated to providing international students with a solid foundation in the Japanese language and culture. With a focus on both practical communication skills and academic preparation, Asuka Academy aims to equip students with the necessary tools to succeed in their future academic and professional endeavors in Japan. The school fosters a supportive and multicultural learning environment, encouraging students to interact with one another and develop a deep appreciation for Japanese society.

## Curriculum & Courses

Asuka Academy offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to cater to students of varying Japanese language proficiency levels. The school provides a range of courses, from beginner to advanced, focusing on developing all four language skills: reading, writing, listening, and speaking. The curriculum also emphasizes Japanese culture and customs, enabling students to gain a deeper understanding of Japanese society.

The school places a strong emphasis on preparing students for the Examination for Japanese University Admission for International Students (EJU) and the Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT). Specialized EJU preparation courses are available for students aiming to pursue higher education in Japan, particularly in science-related fields.
