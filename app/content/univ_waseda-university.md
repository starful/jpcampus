It's known for its strong liberal arts programs, its vibrant international student body, and its significant contributions to research across various fields.

Waseda is a member of the Top Global University Project (SGU) and is widely regarded as one of the 'Big Six' private universities in Japan, alongside Keio University, Sophia University, International Christian University, Meiji University, and Rikkyo University.

The university consistently ranks among the top universities in Asia and globally. Its alumni network includes numerous prominent figures in Japanese politics, business, literature, and academia.

## üåç International Student Support

Waseda University is committed to providing comprehensive support to its international students. This includes:

*   **Dormitory:** Waseda offers a variety of on-campus and off-campus housing options for international students. The International Residence Center provides assistance in finding suitable accommodation. Waseda also manages several international student dormitories.
*   **English Programs:** Many faculties offer courses and programs taught entirely in English, particularly at the graduate level. The School of International Liberal Studies (SILS) is an undergraduate program taught entirely in English.
*   **Career Support:** The Career Center provides career counseling, job search assistance, and internship opportunities for international students.
*   **Scholarships:** Waseda offers a range of scholarships for international students, including merit-based scholarships and need-based scholarships. The university also provides information on external scholarship opportunities.
*   **Student Support Services:** Waseda provides various student support services, including counseling services, health services, and disability services. The International Student Orientation provides new students with essential information about life in Japan and at Waseda.
