## üè´ University Overview

Waseda University, officially known as Waseda Daigaku, is a prestigious private research university located in Tokyo, Japan. Founded in 1882 by ≈åkuma Shigenobu, it is one of the oldest and most influential universities in the country. Waseda is renowned for its academic excellence, diverse student body, and strong alumni network. It consistently ranks among the top universities in Japan and Asia, attracting students and researchers from around the world.

The university's history is deeply intertwined with the modernization of Japan. ≈åkuma Shigenobu, a prominent statesman and advocate for democratic reforms, established Waseda as a progressive institution that welcomed students regardless of their social background. This commitment to inclusivity and academic freedom has shaped Waseda's identity throughout its history. Waseda has played a significant role in shaping Japanese politics, business, and culture, and its graduates have gone on to become leaders in various fields.

Waseda University is committed to fostering global citizenship and promoting international collaboration. It has established numerous partnerships with universities and research institutions worldwide, enabling students and faculty to participate in exchange programs, joint research projects, and other international initiatives. The university offers a wide range of courses and programs taught in English, catering to the needs of international students and fostering a multicultural learning environment. Waseda is also a designated university under the Japanese government's Top Global University Project (SGU), further solidifying its commitment to internationalization.

The university's vibrant campus life offers a wide range of extracurricular activities, clubs, and organizations that cater to diverse interests. From sports teams and cultural societies to academic clubs and volunteer groups, there is something for everyone at Waseda. The university also hosts numerous events and festivals throughout the year, providing students with opportunities to connect with each other and engage with the broader community. Waseda University provides extensive support services for international students, including assistance with visa applications, housing, and cultural adjustment. The university's international student office offers a wide range of programs and services designed to help international students succeed academically and socially.

## üåç International Student Support

Waseda University provides extensive support services for international students to ensure a smooth transition and successful academic experience. These services include:

*   **Dormitory:** Waseda University offers several dormitories specifically for international students. These dormitories provide a safe and comfortable living environment, as well as opportunities to interact with other students from around the world.
*   **English Programs:** Many departments offer courses taught in English. The School of International Liberal Studies (SILS) is fully taught in English. Intensive Japanese language courses are also offered.
*   **Career Support:** The university's Career Center provides career counseling, job search assistance, and internship opportunities for international students. The center also organizes workshops and seminars on topics such as resume writing and interview skills.
*   **Visa Support:** Assistance with visa application and renewal processes.
*   **International Student Advising:** Dedicated advisors to assist with academic and personal matters.
*   **Cultural Exchange Programs:** Opportunities to participate in cultural exchange programs and events.
