## üè´ University Overview

Yokohama National University (YNU), or Yokohama Kokudai, was established in 1949 through the amalgamation of several existing educational institutions in Yokohama, including the Yokohama College of Economics, the Yokohama Technical College, the Yokohama Normal School, and the Kanagawa Normal School for Youth. This merger created a comprehensive university aimed at contributing to the reconstruction of post-war Japan and fostering global perspectives. YNU has since grown into a prominent national university known for its strong emphasis on practical learning and international collaboration.

YNU has a solid reputation, especially in the fields of engineering, economics, and business administration. Its commitment to practical application is reflected in its curriculum, which emphasizes research projects, internships, and collaborations with industry partners. The university is actively involved in international exchange programs, attracting students and researchers from around the world. YNU is dedicated to fostering future leaders who can contribute to solving global challenges.

The university is located in Yokohama, a vibrant port city with a rich history and diverse culture. This location provides students with a unique learning environment, combining academic rigor with urban accessibility. The campus itself is designed to promote interdisciplinary interaction and a sense of community among students and faculty.

## üåç International Student Support

Yokohama National University provides extensive support for international students to ensure a smooth transition and a fulfilling academic experience. This includes:

*   **Dormitory:** YNU offers on-campus housing options specifically for international students. These dormitories provide a convenient and affordable living environment and facilitate interaction with other students from diverse backgrounds. Application details and availability information can be obtained from the International Office.
*   **English Programs:** While many courses are taught in Japanese, YNU offers a growing number of programs and courses in English, particularly at the graduate level. This allows international students with limited Japanese proficiency to pursue their academic interests.
*   **Japanese Language Courses:** For students who wish to improve their Japanese language skills, YNU provides Japanese language courses at various proficiency levels. These courses help students to communicate effectively in daily life and academic settings.
*   **Career Support:** The university's career services department offers guidance and resources to international students seeking employment opportunities in Japan or abroad. This includes resume workshops, interview practice, and job search assistance.
*   **International Student Advising:** Dedicated advisors are available to assist international students with academic, personal, and immigration-related matters. They provide guidance on course selection, visa procedures, and other important aspects of student life.
*   **Buddy Program:** YNU offers a buddy program that pairs international students with local students. This helps international students to adjust to life in Japan and provides opportunities for cultural exchange.
