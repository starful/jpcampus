Subaru Gakuin Motosu School, located in the serene Gifu Prefecture, offers a comprehensive Japanese language education tailored for international students aspiring to pursue higher education in Japan. Unlike schools situated in bustling metropolitan areas, Subaru Gakuin benefits from a tranquil environment conducive to focused study and a lower cost of living. This allows students to concentrate on their academic pursuits without the distractions and financial burdens often associated with larger cities. The school's philosophy centers around providing a supportive and personalized learning experience, ensuring that each student receives the attention and guidance needed to succeed in their academic endeavors. Subaru Gakuin acknowledges that each student possesses distinct academic goals and learning preferences, which is why they provide specialized courses tailored for diverse academic paths. Their commitment extends beyond simple language acquisition, aiming to equip students with the cultural understanding and practical communication skills necessary to thrive both academically and socially in Japan. The school fosters a welcoming and inclusive community, where students from various backgrounds can interact, learn from each other, and build lasting friendships. 

Subaru Gakuin distinguishes itself through its dedicated focus on university and graduate school preparation. Recognizing the specific challenges faced by international students applying to Japanese institutions, the school has meticulously designed its curriculum to address these needs. The core principle is to provide a pathway towards advanced education in Japan. This encompasses comprehensive training in Japanese language skills, but also extends to preparing students for the Examination for Japanese University Admission for International Students (EJU). Subaru Gakuin understands that success on the EJU is paramount for gaining admission to top universities and offers targeted preparation in both liberal arts and STEM subjects. Moreover, the school's faculty consists of experienced educators who are well-versed in the Japanese education system and can provide valuable insights and guidance on the application process. They provide individual consultations, mock interviews, and assistance with preparing application documents, increasing the likelihood of student acceptance into desired programs.

One of the significant advantages of Subaru Gakuin is its commitment to practical Japanese language acquisition. The school emphasizes conversational skills, recognizing that fluency in spoken Japanese is essential for both academic success and everyday life in Japan. Interactive lessons, group discussions, and real-life simulation exercises are integrated into the curriculum to promote active learning and build confidence in speaking Japanese. Furthermore, the school provides opportunities for students to interact with native Japanese speakers through extracurricular activities and cultural exchange programs. This immerses students in the language and culture, enabling them to refine their language skills in a natural and engaging environment. The school also acknowledges the importance of cultural understanding and incorporates elements of Japanese culture into its language lessons, enabling students to better comprehend the nuances of the language and communicate effectively with locals. Ultimately, Subaru Gakuin's teaching methodology seeks to develop well-rounded individuals who are equipped with the linguistic proficiency and cultural sensitivity needed to succeed in Japan.

## üìö Courses, Curriculum & Teaching Style

Subaru Gakuin offers two primary courses tailored to students with different start dates and program durations. The *Japanese University Preparatory 2-Year Course* commences in April, providing a comprehensive two-year program for students seeking in-depth language and academic preparation. This course is meticulously designed to equip students with the language skills, knowledge, and cultural understanding necessary for success in Japanese universities. The *Japanese University Preparatory 1.5-Year Course* starts in October, offering an accelerated yet thorough program for students with some prior Japanese language experience or those who prefer a shorter course duration. Both courses share a common goal: to prepare students for the EJU and university entrance exams. However, the 1.5-year course is more intensive, requiring students to dedicate more time to study and practice. Both courses incorporate the four core language skills: reading, writing, listening, and speaking. Lessons are structured to progressively build upon fundamental grammar and vocabulary, gradually introducing more complex language structures and concepts. Emphasis is placed on developing practical communication skills, enabling students to engage in meaningful conversations and express themselves fluently. The curriculum also encompasses instruction in Japanese culture, customs, and etiquette, fostering a deeper understanding of Japanese society.

Beyond the core language curriculum, Subaru Gakuin offers specialized tracks to cater to students with specific academic interests. These tracks include options for students pursuing art and design, business, and other specialized fields. The *Art and Design* track provides students with the language skills and portfolio development guidance needed to apply to art and design universities in Japan. This involves learning industry-specific vocabulary and techniques, as well as developing the ability to effectively communicate their creative ideas in Japanese. The *Business Japanese* track focuses on developing the language skills needed to succeed in the Japanese business world. This includes learning business terminology, understanding Japanese business etiquette, and practicing professional communication skills. These specialized tracks provide students with a competitive edge when applying to universities and pursuing careers in their chosen fields. This focused approach significantly enhances their prospects of securing admission to reputable institutions and subsequently thriving in their chosen professions. The school also offers comprehensive EJU preparation, providing students with practice tests, mock exams, and personalized feedback. This helps students identify their strengths and weaknesses and develop strategies to improve their performance on the EJU.

Subaru Gakuin's teaching style is characterized by its emphasis on active learning and student participation. Teachers utilize a variety of interactive teaching methods, such as group discussions, role-playing exercises, and project-based learning, to engage students and promote active learning. The school recognizes that different students learn in different ways and strives to create a learning environment that caters to diverse learning styles. Teachers provide individualized attention and support, ensuring that each student receives the help they need to succeed. The school also utilizes technology to enhance the learning experience, incorporating multimedia resources and online learning platforms into the curriculum. This enables students to access learning materials outside of the classroom and engage in self-paced learning.
