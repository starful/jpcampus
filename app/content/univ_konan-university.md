## üè´ University Overview

Konan University, located in Kobe, Japan, is a private university with a rich history and a strong commitment to providing high-quality education. Founded in 1919 by Heisuke Mizushima, Konan University initially started as a private school under the old system. The university emphasizes fostering well-rounded individuals with a global perspective, promoting critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Konan University has grown into a comprehensive institution with multiple faculties and graduate schools, attracting students from all over Japan and around the world.

The university is known for its beautiful campus environment and its dedication to research and innovation. It actively engages in collaborative projects with local and international partners, contributing to advancements in various fields. Konan University's alumni have made significant contributions to business, academia, and public service, reflecting the university's commitment to nurturing future leaders.

Konan University aims to cultivate individuals who can contribute to society by fostering their ability to think for themselves, encouraging their sense of independence, and developing their global awareness. The university also places great importance on international exchange and offers various programs to promote intercultural understanding and global citizenship.

## üåç International Student Support

Konan University provides various support services for international students to ensure a smooth and enriching academic experience. These services include:

*   **Dormitory:** On-campus and off-campus housing options are available for international students. The university provides assistance in finding suitable accommodation.
*   **English Programs:** Many courses are offered in English, particularly in the Faculty of Global Communication and other specialized programs. Japanese language courses are also available to help international students improve their language skills.
*   **Career Support:** The university's career center provides guidance and resources to international students seeking internships and job opportunities in Japan and abroad. They offer career counseling, resume workshops, and job search support.
*   **International Student Advisors:** Dedicated advisors are available to assist international students with visa issues, academic advising, and general support.
*   **Cultural Exchange Programs:** The university organizes various cultural exchange programs and events to promote intercultural understanding and interaction between international and Japanese students.
