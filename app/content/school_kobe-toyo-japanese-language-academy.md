## School Overview

Kobe Toyo Japanese Language Academy, situated in the vibrant city of Kobe, Hyogo Prefecture, offers a comprehensive Japanese language education for international students. The academy prides itself on fostering a welcoming and supportive learning environment where students can thrive academically and culturally. With a long history of successful language education, the school focuses on helping students achieve their individual goals, whether it's university entrance, career advancement, or simply improving their Japanese language skills for personal enrichment.

The academy's philosophy centers around providing a balanced curriculum that combines grammar, vocabulary, reading, writing, listening, and speaking skills. Emphasis is placed on practical communication skills to ensure students can confidently use Japanese in real-life situations. The campus atmosphere is friendly and multicultural, encouraging interaction between students from various backgrounds and fostering a global perspective.

## Curriculum & Courses

The academy offers a structured curriculum designed to cater to students of all levels, from complete beginners to advanced learners. The courses are carefully designed to align with the Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) levels, ensuring students are well-prepared for the exam. Furthermore, the curriculum covers subjects crucial for students aiming to take the Examination for Japanese University Admission for International Students (EJU), particularly those pursuing science-related fields.

The academy provides both long-term and short-term courses, catering to varying learning objectives and time commitments. The long-term courses are designed for students aiming for academic advancement, while the short-term courses are suitable for those who want to experience Japanese culture and improve their conversational skills.
