Privacy Policy

JP Campus ("the Site") values user privacy and complies with relevant privacy laws.

1. Information Collection

You can view content without registration. However, when using the 'Contact' feature, we collect:

  • Items: Name, Email address, Message content.
  • Method: Collected via the contact form (linked with Google Apps Script).

2. Purpose of Collection

Collected information is used solely for:

  • Replying to inquiries and feedback.
  • Improving our service based on user opinions.

3. Cookies & Ads

We use cookies to provide better services.

  • Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense for revenue. Google and third-party vendors use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

4. Retention & Destruction

Personal information is destroyed without delay once the purpose of collection is achieved.

5. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please use our contact page.
