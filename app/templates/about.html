About JP Campus

Welcome to JP Campus, the premier data-driven platform dedicated to empowering international students in their journey to study in Japan. We understand that choosing to study abroad is one of the most significant decisions in your life. It is not just about learning a language; it is about choosing a new environment, a potential career path, and a future home.

Navigating the landscape of Japanese education can be daunting. With hundreds of language schools and universities scattered across the archipelago, finding reliable, comparable, and transparent information is a challenge. JP Campus was born out of a desire to solve this information asymmetry. We believe that every student deserves access to clear, accurate, and visualized data to make informed decisions without the noise of aggressive marketing.

üîç Data Methodology & Transparency

At JP Campus, data integrity is our core value. We do not accept payments from schools to alter their rankings or hide data. Our information is aggregated from:

  • Official School Websites: We crawl and verify tuition fees, course offerings, and facility details directly from the source.
  • Association for the Promotion of Japanese Language Education (Nisshinkyo): We utilize public datasets regarding school capacity, student demographics, and legal compliance status.
  • AI-Enhanced Processing: We employ advanced AI models (LLMs) to standardize unstructured text into comparable metrics, ensuring that a "University Prep Course" in Tokyo means the same data point as one in Osaka.

While we make every effort to ensure accuracy, school policies and fees can change rapidly. We always recommend visiting the official website of the institution through the links provided on our detail pages for the most up-to-date information.
