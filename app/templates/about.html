About JP Campus

JP Campus is a data platform designed for international students preparing to study in Japan. We provide map-based information on Japanese language schools across the country.

Finding the right school among hundreds of options can be overwhelming. We visualize key data such as location, tuition fees, nationality ratios, and admission records to help you find the best fit quickly and easily.

Our mission is to support the first step of your successful study abroad journey by providing a customized search environment tailored to your goals (university advancement, employment, conversation, etc.), rather than just listing schools.
